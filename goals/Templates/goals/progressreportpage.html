Chart your daily progress and see the results in the long run

Whether you're trying to lose weight or gain muscle, the only way to know for sure is by keeping track of your workouts each time you hit the gym. Keeping a log doesn't stop at the gym, it is also essential to keep track of your meal plan because exercising is only 35% of the work, the 65% comes from eating a healthy diet and resting. Not to mention, but keeping a log is also a great confidence booster when you look back and see how far you've come along. Remember, you are not challenging others at the gym, the only person you are challenging is yourself! Do not try lifting heavier than you can handle or you will risk the chance of injury.
